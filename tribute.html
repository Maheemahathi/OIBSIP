Mahendra Singh Dhoni - The Captain Cool

7 July,1981

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, known for his calmness under pressure, is one of the greatest cricket captains of all time.

About Captain Cool

2004: Dhoni made his international debut in ODI against Bangladesh.

2007: He led India to victory in the inaugural ICC T20 World Cup.

20011: Under his captaincy, India won the ICC Cricket World Cup after 28 years..

2013: Dhoni captained India to victory in the ICC Champions Trophy.

2016: Stepped down as the captain of India's limited-overs teams.

2020: Announced retirement from international cricket, ending a glorious career.
